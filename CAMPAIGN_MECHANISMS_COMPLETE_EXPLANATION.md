## 🚀 **How Both Campaign Types Work**

### **1. 🤖 AI-Generated Campaigns**

**Workflow:**
```
User Input → AI Generation → Preview → Approve → Save → Send
```

**Process Details:**
1. **User fills AI form**: Business name, campaign type, prompt, tone, target audience
2. **AI generates content**: Backend calls AI services (OpenAI/Groq/Gemini/Claude) 
3. **Structured response**: AI returns JSON with campaign_title, message_sequence, etc.
4. **Preview**: Shows professionally formatted content with WhatsApp-style preview
5. **Approve**: User approves and saves to database with `generatedContent.jsonStructure`
6. **Send**: WhatsApp client delivers the AI-generated messages

**Content Source:** 
- ✅ **100% AI-generated** based on your business context and prompts
- ✅ Uses your business data for personalization
- ✅ Follows professional marketing patterns

### **2. 📝 Manual Campaigns**

**Workflow:**
```
User Input → Preview → Save → Send
```

**Process Details:**
1. **User writes everything**: Campaign name, description, content, selects contacts
2. **No AI involvement**: Content goes directly as-is unless you click "Generate AI Content"  
3. **Preview**: Shows your manual content in WhatsApp-style format
4. **Save**: Stores in database with your exact content
5. **Send**: WhatsApp client delivers your exact messages

**Content Source:**
- ✅ **100% your manual input** - NO AI unless you specifically request it
- ✅ Complete control over messaging
- ✅ Optional AI enhancement available
